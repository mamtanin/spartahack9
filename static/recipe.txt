(Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry with Rice)

(Ingredients)
1. 2 Grilled Chicken Breasts (diced)
2. 2 Hamburger Patties (diced)
3. 2 Carrots (sliced)
4. 1 Tomato (chopped)
5. 1 Orange (juiced)
6. 2 cups of Rice
7. 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
8. Salt and Pepper to taste
9. 1 teaspoon of Olive Oil
10. 1 tablespoon of Sesame seeds
11. Half head of Lettuce (shredded)

(Steps)
1. Cook rice according to package instructions, then set aside.
2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
3. Add the diced grilled chicken breasts and the hamburger patties to the pan and cook until they're browned and cooked through. 
4. Add the sliced carrots and cook, stirring frequently, until they're tender.
5. Add the chopped tomato to the skillet, continue cooking until the tomatoes begin to soften.
6. Pour the orange juice and soy sauce over the cooked ingredients in the skillet, stir to coat everything evenly in the sauce.
7. Lower the heat, and let the stir fry simmer for a few minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.
8. Season the stir fry with salt and pepper to taste.
9. Serve the stir fry over the cooked rice. 
10. Garnish with the shredded lettuce and a sprinkle of sesame seeds before serving. 

This recipe combines a variety of flavors and textures for a dish that's not only tasty, but also packed full of nutritious ingredients. Enjoy this Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry with Rice for a wholesome and satisfying meal!
